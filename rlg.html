Random Logic Games

motion design

Random Logic Games is an app game company based in Birmingham Alabama. In the spring of 2019, during my senior year of college, I worked for them as a remote part-time motion designer. I created animated advertisements for their infinite word search games. These advertisements included recorded gameplay, pre-made assets (such as the endcard, which I did not make) and new assets. I created the illustrations featured in the backgrounds of the advertisements, and found the imagery and background music.
