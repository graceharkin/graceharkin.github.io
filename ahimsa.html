Ahimsa Hair Care Professionals

brand identity // print // packaging // publication

Ahimsa is a hypothetical hair and skin beauty company that specializes in making and maintaining customized wigs, specifically for hair loss diseases such as Alopecia. This project was an undergraduate studio project in which we got to ideate a company and create their branding and stationery suite. Ahimsa’s services expand beyond wigs to provide regular hair care such as cutting and coloring, skin care such as lotions and shampoos, and consultation services. Ahimsa stands out from other beauty companies because of its specialized yet diverse services. Ahimsa is known for their beauty campaign focusing on the destigmatization of hair loss, promoting healthy, natural beauty care, and mental well-being.

The three tiers of Ahimsa’s services include hair services such as cutting, coloring, and creating customized wigs (represented with pink), skin services such as lotions and ointments for the scalp and neck regions to sooth skin conditions (represented with blue), and consultation services to help customers find exactly what they need to make them feel confident and comfortable (represented in yellow). The colors on packaing correlates with the service area of the product. Business cards and stationary suites are also color coded based on specialization area.

Ahimsa’s brand guidelines book provides extensive guidance on everything from design standards, such as logo, color, typography, and imagery usage to to customer service standards and the company mission.
